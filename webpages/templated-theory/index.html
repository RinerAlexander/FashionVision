What's in your closet?

Digitize your wardrobe and find the best outfit for you

Inspiration

In this digital age, consumers are always seeking ways to simplify and organize their lives. That is where closet apps come in! There are hundreds of closets apps available for mobile and digital platforms that offer the promise of organizing your wardrobe for easy access and visibility to plan daily outfits for long periods. Track clothing use to help calculate your cost/wear and declutter your closet by identifying outfits rarely worn for easy disposal. An examination of the top 5 apps in the Apple App store offers the ability to mass upload pictures of clothing and other features, there is a fundamental opportunity yet to be explored. In order to get the app ready, the user has to manually tag and classify each item and separate them into respective folders.
