Image Upload

Here is where you can upload an image to try out the Fashion Vision model. Keep in mind that the model is very sensitive and relies on the shape of your picture to identify your clothing. With that in mind please abide by the following guidelines to help the computer accuately identify your picture.

White Background

Take your picture in front of a white background with just the article you want identified.

Capture the Shape

FashionVision works by identifying the shape of your clothing, as such try to fully capture the shape in your picture for a more accurate prediciton.
